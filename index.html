Gulab Jamun

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Oil
  • Sugar

Recipe

  1. Mix milk powder, all-purpose flour, ghee, and a pinch of baking soda in a bowl.
  2. Gradually add milk and knead to form a smooth, soft dough.
  3. Divide the dough into small portions, shape them into balls, and deep fry until golden brown.

Barfi

Ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Sugar

Recipe

  1. Heat ghee in a pan and add grated khoya. Cook on low heat until it softens and starts to leave the sides of the pan..
  2. Add sugar and continue to cook, stirring continuously, until the mixture thickens and forms a dough-like consistency.
  3. Optionally, mix in chopped nuts or cardamom powder for flavor. Transfer the mixture to a greased tray and let it set.
